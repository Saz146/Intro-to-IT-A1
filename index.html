Overview

The Information Technology project will be a fitness tracker. The fitness tracker will track the fitness progress of the individual as they continue to train for a period of time. The project will be made using a programming language such as Python or through a tool such as an app inventor. This idea is innovative and will be very useful to gym attendees. Each time the individual finishes a set, they will enter the details of the set, such as the weight and reps of an exercise. The information will be entered using speech-to-text which is an innovative way of inputting information. After the individual records the information, then they can save it. In their next session they can see their previous workout information. By doing this, the individual can track their progression and see if they are achieving their fitness goals.

Motivation

The project will be useful for gym goers because it will help them with their fitness goals. If the gym goer knows how much they have improved in their fitness; it will satisfy them. The goal of this application is to satisfy its customers, and by enabling valuable information to be recorded, the goal will be reached. This project will be important because a survey by Finder revealed that in 2020, around 6.2 million (32%) have a gym membership. This highlights that there would be a substantial market for a fitness tracker with a speech-to-text function. The project is interesting because it is a new take on fitness apps. Fitness tracker apps are not known for having voice recognition so this will be something original.

Features

As stated before, this application will be produced using a programming language or an app inventor. These factors are important because the product can not be made without them. The product will have a blue background to increase the appearance of the app. Since the appearance is so crucial, it is important that the design of the app is sophisticated. A blue background would look much better than a blank background. If the background where to be blank, then it would be dull. The avoidance of colour would decrease the user’s desire to use the app. Therefore, colour will be used in the application so that the design looks appealing and vivid. The use of menus and buttons is used throughout many websites and applications worldwide. Menus and buttons give the user a sense of orientation, which means that the users will always know where to go depending on what they want to do. The fitness tracker app will have menus and buttons in it so that it is easier for the user to navigate through the application. The app will also record the date of the workout once the workout is entered into the system. The product will have a “Previous Stats” menu, where the individual can see the information that they have previously stored on the application, to compare it with their recent progress. There will also be an “Add Stats” menu where the individual can add their most recent workout information and then save it. This information will be stored in the “Previous Stats Menu” after it is saved. There will be an “Add Workout” button where the individual will be able to utilise the speech-to-text function to input their workout information. There will also be a “Delete” button so that the individual can remove workout information if they desire to. There will also be two “Return” buttons so that the individual can go back to the main menu. There will be an “Exit” button so that the user can leave the program at any time they wish. It is crucial for there to be an exit button as the user must not be restricted and they must always have the freedom to leave the application whenever they please.
